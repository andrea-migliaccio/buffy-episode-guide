# Season 2 Overview

Season 2 deepens the emotional and narrative complexity of *Buffy the Vampire Slayer*.  
It marks the arrival of two iconic characters — **Spike** and **Drusilla** — and focuses heavily on Buffy’s evolving relationship with Angel.

Key themes and arcs:
- Introduction of Spike & Drusilla as major antagonists  
- Buffy and Angel’s romance, culminating in a devastating twist  
- Angel's transformation into **Angelus**, the season's Big Bad  
- Exploration of identity, consequences, and sacrifice  
- Expansion of the Slayer mythology (e.g., introduction of Kendra)  

Season 2 is widely considered one of the show’s strongest, with several essential episodes for the overarching story.
