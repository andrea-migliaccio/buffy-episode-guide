# Season 4 Overview

Season 4 marks a major transition for Buffy and the Scoobies as they leave high school behind and begin navigating adulthood, independence, and shifting relationships.  
The season introduces **the Initiative**, a secret government organization studying and weaponizing demons, and its mysterious leader **Professor Maggie Walsh**.  

The primary antagonist eventually becomes **Adam**, a cybernetic demon-human hybrid created by the Initiative.  
This season also explores deeper character arcs:
- Buffy adapting to college life and a new romantic relationship with **Riley Finn**
- Willow’s growing magical abilities and the beginning of her relationship with **Tara**
- Giles' struggle with his post-Watcher identity
- The group learning how to function in a more adult world

The season mixes monster-of-the-week episodes with broader explorations of identity, government control, and the nature of evil.  
While sometimes considered uneven, Season 4 contains crucial lore and character developments.
