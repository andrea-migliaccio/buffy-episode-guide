# Season 7 Overview

Season 7 brings *Buffy the Vampire Slayer* to its final arc, returning to core themes of destiny, empowerment, and the nature of the Slayer line.  
The Big Bad of the season is **The First Evil**, the oldest and purest form of evil, capable of taking the appearance of any dead person.  

Key elements and arcs:
- The First Evil’s assault on the Slayer lineage  
- The introduction of the **Potential Slayers**, who gather in Sunnydale  
- Buffy stepping fully into a leadership role  
- Spike’s redemption arc, driven by his newly regained soul  
- The rise of the **Turok-Han**, primal vampires linked to the First  
- The Watchers’ Council being destroyed  
- The emotional and ideological fractures within the Scooby Gang  
- A finale centered on **changing the Slayer myth forever**, empowering all potentials  

Season 7 is thematically dense, endgame-focused, and sets up a massive ensemble leading to the final battle under Sunnydale.
