# Season 3 Overview

Season 3 focuses on identity, autonomy, and the consequences of past actions.  
It introduces one of the most beloved and complex characters in the Buffyverse: **Faith**, the new Slayer activated after Kendra’s death.  
The season also sets up major plot threads that will affect Buffy and Angel for years to come — including Angel’s departure toward his own path.

Main arcs and themes:
- **Faith**: her arrival, fall from grace, and eventual alliance with the Mayor  
- **The Mayor**: one of the show’s most iconic Big Bads, with a long-term plan to ascend into a pure demon  
- Buffy’s struggle for independence and identity beyond the Slayer mantle  
- The tensions between Buffy and the Watchers’ Council  
- The final emotional steps that lead Angel to leave Sunnydale  
- Senior year: choices, transitions, and the looming future  

Season 3 is widely considered one of the strongest in the series, with several foundational episodes.
