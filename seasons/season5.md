# Season 5 Overview

Season 5 marks a major turning point in *Buffy the Vampire Slayer*, both narratively and emotionally.  
It introduces **Dawn**, Buffy’s mysteriously sudden younger sister, and builds toward one of the most powerful finales of the entire series.

Major elements include:
- Dawn’s arrival and the reveal of her true nature as **The Key**  
- **Glory (Glorificus)**, a hell-god and one of the most dangerous villains Buffy ever faces  
- Buffy’s deepening struggles with adult responsibility, loss, and identity  
- The growing frustration between the Watchers’ Council and the Scoobies  
- Spike’s evolving feelings for Buffy, shifting him toward a more complex moral alignment  
- The emotional weight of **Joyce’s illness** and the impact of grief  

Season 5 is widely considered one of the show's most emotionally devastating and thematically rich seasons, culminating in Buffy's ultimate act of sacrifice.
