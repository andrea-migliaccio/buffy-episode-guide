# Season 1 Overview

Buffy arrives in Sunnydale and quickly discovers that the town sits on the **Hellmouth**, a supernatural hotspot attracting demons, vampires, and dark forces.  
This season introduces the core cast (Buffy, Giles, Willow, Xander, Cordelia, Angel) and establishes the central mythology of the Slayer.  

The main storyline follows:
- Buffy’s struggle to accept her role as the Slayer  
- The rise of **The Master**, the season’s Big Bad  
- Early hints about the Hellmouth and its influence  
- The foundations of the Buffy/Angel relationship  

Season 1 is short (mid-season replacement) and heavily focused on setting up the world and tone of the show.
